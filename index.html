✦ How It Works

Prompt to Movie in Minutes

Our 23-step AI pipeline handles everything automatically while you stay in control.

1
Enter Your Prompt
Type a short idea — "A brave boy saves a lost puppy in the mountains." Choose style, genre, length, voice, and aspect ratio.
2
AI Builds Your Story World
Full story, character profiles, story bible, environment references, and scene breakdown — all generated and locked for consistency.
3
Scenes, Images & Videos Generated
Every scene gets a cinematic image, an animated video clip, AI voiceover, and synced subtitles — automatically.
4
Edit in Timeline, Export in 4K
Fine-tune in the professional timeline editor, add music, adjust subtitles, then export in MP4/MOV at up to 4K resolution.
